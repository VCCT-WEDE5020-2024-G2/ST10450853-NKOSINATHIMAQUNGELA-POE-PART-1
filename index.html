What we offer

We offer the people of Cape Town, that have been affected by natural disasters, emergency services, shelter, and the knowledge they need to be prepared for any disasters. Many homes throughout the Western Province have succumed to the brutal forces of nature, and many of the people that were affected, do not have the finances to start afresh. This is why we offer them shelter at many of our locations throughout Cape Town.
